GBM Simulator

Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM) is a stochastic process used to model the random behavior of financial assets, such as currency exchange rates and stock prices. It assumes that the logarithm of the asset's price follows a Brownian motion with constant drift and volatility. GBM is widely used in finance to simulate and predict price movements over time.
